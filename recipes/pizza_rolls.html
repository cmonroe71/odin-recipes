Pizza Rolls

Description

This mashed potatoes recipe comes from my mother. She also used irish butter and cream cheese instead of milk!

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • 1 tbsp Irish Butter
  • 1 tbsp Cream Cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel Potatoes
  2. Boil Water
  3. Mash Potatoes
  4. add butter, cream cheese, salt and pepper
  5. Stir and mash
