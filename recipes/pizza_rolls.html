Pizza Rolls

Description

These pizza rolls are almost as good as Totinos! Enjoy them while watching any wrestling PPV!!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups shredded pizza cheese blend or part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 lb bulk Italian sausage, cooked and drained
  • 2 packages (3 oz each) sliced pepperoni, chopped
  • 1 medium green pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 medium sweet red pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 jars (14 oz each) pizza sauce
  • 32 egg roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying
  • Additional pizza sauce for dipping

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cheese sausage, pepperoni, and onion.
  2. Stir in pizza sauce until combined.
  3. Place about 1/4 filling in the center of each egg roll wrapper.
  4. Fold bottom corner over filling; fold sides toward center over filling
  5. Moisten remaining corner with water and roll up tightly to seal.
  6. In a Dutch oven or deep cast-iron skillet, heat 1-2 in. of oil to 375 degrees.
  7. Fry pizza rolls until goledn brown, 1-2 minutes on each side
  8. Drain on paper towels and serve with additional pizza sauce
